01

Story

Biomedical researcher with over four years of experience at OHSU's Vaccine and Gene Therapy Institute, specializing in human HIV immunology and nonhuman primate SIV models. My work has been conducted under the mentorship of Dr. Hiroshi Takata in the laboratories of Dr. Lydie Trautmann and Dr. Afam Okoye, with recent collaboration alongside Dr. David Ho's team at Columbia University. Driven by a passion for translational science, I earned a Master's degree in Data Science from Willamette University to integrate machine learning and modern analytical methods into biomedical research. My goal is to leverage computational biology, immunology, and data science to accelerate drug discovery and advance the development of next-generation therapeutics.
